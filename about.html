About Us

A family-owned eatery and washery founded in Eugene, MO in 2020

We built this restaraunt to create not only a restaraunt, but an asset to the community. The carwash was made to bring a needed commodity closer to home.

We wanted a place close to home to find good authentic Cajun food, and a place for the community to come together. With imported food from Louisana and daily specials, we're sure to have what you're looking for!
